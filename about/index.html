About

I started this project after getting laid off from a job and hoping to find a new career path. I’ve always been an avid sports fan and have been particularly interested in the strange parts of sports - the parts where the best athletes in the world look just like you and me. So I set out to see if I could quantify the strangest parts of sports. I hope you’ll join me on this journey. If you’d like to message me you can reach me on Twitter or send me an email at bendemouth@gmail.com
